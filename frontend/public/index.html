UpGrade

AI-Powered Study Assistant

UpGrade is a student productivity application designed to help users organize their academic work by securely connecting to Google Classroom. After signing in with Google, users can view their enrolled courses, assignments, and deadlines in one unified dashboard.

UpGrade accesses Google Classroom data in read-only mode. The app does not modify courses, submit assignments, post content, or perform any actions on behalf of users.

The purpose of UpGrade is to help students plan their workload, track upcoming deadlines, and improve academic organization.
